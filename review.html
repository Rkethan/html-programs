Movie Review

Movie Name:RRR

Director:SS rajamouli

Released date:24 March 2022

Rating: 5.0 Out of 5

From Actors JR ntr & Ramcharan

Highlights:

  • The movie starts with an high intense scenes of introductions from both the heroes.
  • The first half of the film is highly impressive based on the friendship between jr ntr and ramcharan
  • The music was extrodinary from the MM keeravani
  • Screenplay was well executed by the director ss rajamouli
  • BGM played key role in making each and every scene more effective

Finally,Congratulations SS rajamouli sir..The way u deal with RRR subject was superb..JR ntr & Ramcharan(the faces of the indian cinema) given their best performance in rrr movie.
